Medbarim Shlichut by Asi Aharonov PhD.

Welcome to the podcast Medabrim Shlichut (literally - Talking Mission) - the only podcast that gives a stage to Israeli Schlichim.
We interview past and present Schlichim who share their experiences with us.
A lot of their experiences are mutual, though many are unique.
Wether you consider going on a Shlichut, are on a Shlichut or were on a Shlichut - you will find here inspiration, important insights and useful tips through the Schlichim's stories.
Everywhere you are, in Israel or abroad, on foot or in your car - you are invited to listen to the podcast that talks with the Schlichim eye to eye
